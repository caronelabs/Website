# Public Assets

Place the following files in this directory:

## Required Assets

1. **logo.png** - Your Carone Labs logo (the CL monogram with "CARONE LABS" text)
   - Recommended dimensions: 320x96 pixels or larger
   - Transparent background preferred

2. **favicon.ico** - Browser tab icon
   - You can generate this from your logo at https://favicon.io/

## Optional Assets

- **og-image.png** - Social media sharing preview (1200x630 pixels)
- **apple-touch-icon.png** - iOS home screen icon (180x180 pixels)
